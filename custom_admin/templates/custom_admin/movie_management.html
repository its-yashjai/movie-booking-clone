Movie Management System

Add, manage, and organize movies across your cinema chain

How It Works

The movie management system follows a structured hierarchy to organize your cinema operations:

System Hierarchy
Movie (e.g., "Avengers: Endgame")
└─ Theater (e.g., "PVR Cyber Hub, Bangalore")
    └─ Screen (e.g., "Screen 1 - IMAX")
        └─ Showtime (e.g., "2025-01-15, 09:30 PM")

Step-by-Step Guide

1 Create Movie

Add basic movie details: title, duration, genre, language, certificate, and poster image.

2 Select Theater

Choose which theater locations will show this movie from your cinema chain.

3 Pick Screen

Select specific screens (auditoriums) within chosen theaters. Each screen has its own seat layout.

4 Create Showtimes

Add show times with dates, start/end times, pricing, and available seats. Multiple showtimes per screen/day.

5 Launch & Monitor

Publish the movie and monitor bookings, seats, and revenue in real-time from the dashboard.

6 Manage & Update

Update pricing, manage availability, track bookings, and handle special requests from the management panel.

Pricing Strategy

Set different prices per showtime. Premium shows (morning/evening) can have different rates than matinee shows.

Seat Management

Each screen has a total seat count. Available seats decrease automatically as bookings increase.
